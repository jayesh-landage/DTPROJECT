Kumbhmela 2027

About Kumbhmela

The Kumbh Mela is the world’s largest religious gathering, held every 12 years at four sacred river-bank pilgrimage sites in India: Prayagraj, Haridwar, Nashik, and Ujjain. Millions of pilgrims gather to bathe in the holy rivers, which is believed to cleanse sins and bring spiritual liberation. Kumbhmela 2027 is expected to be one of the biggest in history, attracting devotees, tourists, and scholars from across the globe.
